FAQ

Q: Offline? What if I’m in a remote area or have no WiFi/ LGE service available?

A: Yes. AmbuSoft works seamlessly online AND offline.

If your device is offline AmbuSoft will save locally to your browser, and the next time your device regains internet connectivity, your reports will be uploaded automatically in the background.

Q: Can the data be accessed from anywhere, is there a way to limit access?

A: Yes. AmbuSoft is cloud based so by default you can access the application from any device anywhere at anytime. In addition, AmbuSoft offers an optional security layer where the system administrator has the ability to lockdown the usage to only agency approved devices.

Q: What happens if someone deletes a patient record, is there historical versioning?

A: Yes. The AmbuSoft system is engineered from the ground up to never lose or delete any data, the report is always versioned or possibly disabled, but the patient report data is always retrievable and never deleted from the database.

Q: Is there logging of user actions such as viewing, editing, and printing?

A: Yes. AmbuSoft logs everything from creation and editing of patient data to actions by users like viewing and printing. These logs can be viewed and managed by admins and exported into excel if necessary.
